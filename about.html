Personal Statement

My interest in media developed when I watched TV series broadcast on TVB Jade Channel at a young age. At that time, I not only learned Cantonese through television but gradually realized that media played a critical role in our daily life and influenced our opinion on social phenomena. Besides, influenced by my uncle and father, who were fond of watching Star Wars and collecting Garage Kits, I started to get exposure to audio-visual language and was curious about film production. Therefore, I want to learn more about media knowledge and its application in authentic cases professionally. In this case, I decided to apply for the Communication at The HKBU.
As a diligent undergraduate student at Sichuan University of Media and Communications, I had made sufficient preparations to pursue postgraduate studies in New Media. Concretely, the course Five Steps to New Media Marketing made me familiar with new media platforms and content marketing, which was beneficial to cultivate my competence in marketing and content production. In addition, Fundamentals of Director fostered my taste for humanity and art and helped me master the basic principles of creation and audio-visual expression. Benefiting from this course, I was adept at using software like Adobe Audition, Adobe Premiere Pro, and DaVinci Resolve. Through learning European and American Film Culture, I could comprehend the concept of film language and narrative and mastered the skills of effectively presenting the natural world and stories via appropriate films. Thus, the academic study equipped me with enough ability to produce reasonable texts and understand the conventions of different genres, paving the way for analyzing media texts from a unique and refreshing insight.
Driven this passion and appreciation for new media, I actively sought opportunities to increase my training in the industry and hone my skills. Concretely, I operated Xiaohongshu Account called Toongkk, which concentrated on current events and hot culture to attract more audiences. As for my responsibilities, I took charge of content planning with suitable soft advertisements to achieve satisfactory promotion. I strictly controlled the shooting materials to attract more fans, which needed to keep in touch with reality to increase the account’s influence. More importantly, this meaningful practice boosted my sharp mind towards hot spots and enhanced my data analysis capability by analyzing background data to adjust operation projects. Additionally, I wrote, directed, edited, and produced the maiden work, a microfilm named Flying to the Moon. In terms of production, I encountered a tricky issue of funding shortage. To settle this problem, I communicated with the equipment staff and suggested they sponsor us. In return, our members could popularize the use of this facility. Moreover, I was in charge of content production to reflect different facets of human lives, immensely strengthening my writing ability. From my perspective, filmmaking was just like a media platform, which adopted various media devices to demonstrate people’s life and thoughts through sounds and images. Furthermore, I acted as an intern at the 10th China Internet Audio & Video Convention, where I was involved in site activity execution. I was responsible for ensuring the smooth running of the activity and inviting the honored guests and popular media to accept an interview. When confronting the lack of audience, I was requested to discover 20 spectators to enhance their interaction with distinguished guests. To solve this thorny matter, I tried communicating with nearby university teachers to organize 20 college students to participate in this activity. It was worth mentioning that this event profoundly cultivated my organizing and communication capabilities. Remarkably, I was fortunate to attend the significant forum, which fostered my new perception towards the development of the future media industry, laying a solid foundation for further study in this field.
At this point, I know I need additional education to accomplish my objective, and the MSc in New Media program at The Chinese University of Hong Kong perfectly aligns with my interests and is a solid link between my current academic passions and future learning aspirations due to its curriculum and faculty. While in the program, I plan to take multiple modules, such as New Media Content Development, New Media Content Development, Understanding Culture Thro Film, and New Communication Technologies: Trends and Impacts, to provide me with a broad understanding of interpersonal communication, organizational communication, computer-mediated communication, and intercultural communication theories. Notably, I will have a precious opportunity to receive hands-on training in multimedia design, development, user interface design, and data analytics to accelerate my practical competence and accumulate more industry experience. Based on the knowledge acquired from classroom and extracurricular practice, I can conduct content and audience research and apply this knowledge in designing strategic information campaigns using new media devices. Above all, this advanced program is conducive to further enhancing my academic study and comprehensive abilities and gaining a deeper understanding of new media and its value. It can help me in my future endeavors.
After completing my master’s degree and obtaining the necessary knowledge in new media, I plan to seek employment for a few years with a firm that will enable me to acquire the required hands-on practice. More specifically, I will engage in content output or operation of the related content during my tenure to broaden my horizon. Based on the accumulated knowledge and industry experience, I will establish an MCN media company where I devote myself to promoting qualified content with massive Internet hits. Beyond that, I will create the image of virtual idols and online celebrities to launch peripheral products.
